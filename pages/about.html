About Us

Introduction

  1. Vision
    • With respect to take the responsibility of protecting the Earth's natural resources, we hope to reduce the felling of trees, to reduce people’s reliance on the plastic products. In 1991, we try to use the fiber of agricultural wastes (rice hull, coconut shell, rice shaft, corn shaft) as the primary raw materials, deployed using local general starch (not the main food ，such as cassava, tuber, etc..） And proceed with our exploitation product (Addition composite) after many of repeated trial, finally developed the product--Fiber Particulate Composite（FPC for short）; then we specialized in design of the sheet pre-forming machine to produce --Fiber Slice Composite（FSC for short） for manufacturing vacuum thermoforming packaging material.
    • In recent years, the environmental protection consciousness gradually gained ground, it was no longer slogan; the environmental protection, the afforested earth, leaving our descendants a clean non-pollution life space, have already been the most important responsibility for us this generation.
    • As a result of FPC producing technology, the concurrent characteristic of plastics and the lumber product; the product has not only highly the environmental protection producing process but reusable.
    • Mainly take Agricultural wastes as raw materials, combined with the non-staple food of biodegradable materials, the compound key training programs, production of substitution of alternative to paper and plastic wood materials.
  2. We demonstrate the principles of 3R:
    • Reduce: less woods would be logged.
    • Recycle: products could be recycled.
    • Replace: rubber, plastic and wood materials could bereplaced.
